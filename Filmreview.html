First filmreview

The first frame of the film is a slogan: “Dreams are messages from the depths of the heart.” This aphorism comes from Dr. Keynes. The practice of presenting the slogan before the factory mark is quite rare in Hollywood films, indicating that the film has a strong authorial attribute.
